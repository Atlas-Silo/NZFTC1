ASCII ER Diagram (simplified)

[Employee] (EmployeeId PK)
 - EmployeeId (int) PK
 - FirstName (string)
 - LastName (string)
 - Email (string) UNIQUE
 - Role (string)
 - DateHired (datetime)
 - Salary (decimal)

[LeaveRequest] (LeaveRequestId PK)
 - LeaveRequestId (int) PK
 - EmployeeId (int) FK -> Employee.EmployeeId
 - StartDate (date)
 - EndDate (date)
 - Type (string)
 - Status (string)
 - RequestedOn (datetime)

[Payroll] (PayrollId PK)
 - PayrollId (int) PK
 - EmployeeId (int) FK -> Employee.EmployeeId
 - PayPeriodStart (date)
 - PayPeriodEnd (date)
 - GrossPay (decimal)
 - Tax (decimal)
 - NetPay (decimal)
 - GeneratedOn (datetime)

[Grievance] (GrievanceId PK)
 - GrievanceId (int) PK
 - EmployeeId (int) FK -> Employee.EmployeeId
 - SubmittedOn (datetime)
 - Subject (string)
 - Details (string)
 - Status (string)

[Holiday] (HolidayId PK)
 - HolidayId (int) PK
 - Date (date)
 - Name (string)

Relationships:
 Employee 1 --- * LeaveRequest
 Employee 1 --- * Payroll
 Employee 1 --- * Grievance
 Holiday standalone
